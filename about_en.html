Cheng-Hong Li | Levi

  • Cheng-Hong Li (Levi) is an agronomy researcher specializing in genetics and rice quality. His previous research projects include:
  • Breeding research to improve japonica rice disease resistance in Taitung region
  • Research on key rice cultivation techniques in central Taiwan
  • Research on climate adaptation cultivation techniques for rice in central Taiwan
  • Research on fertilizer management techniques to mitigate the impact of high temperatures on rice quality in central Taiwan

Education & Experience

  • National Chung Hsing University, Department of Agronomy Master
  • Assistant, Acting, Crop Science Division, Taiwan Agricultural Research Institute (Rice, Corn)
  • Assistant Researcher, Rice Research Laboratory, Taitung District Agricultural Research and Extension Station
  • Assistant Researcher, Rice and Rice Quality Research Laboratory, Taichung District Agricultural Research and Extension Station
